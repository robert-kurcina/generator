Team Generation

The Team.js module provides the attribute to generate a character of quality q or with dice modifier m. The syntax is as follows: Team.getTeam(); //generate a completely random character
Team.getTeam(n); //generate n completely random characters
Specific sets of entirely random characters - as in a team - can also be created by passing in a definition string: var resultObj = Team.getTeam('1xMilitary, 2xSurvivor' or '3xGanger');
alert(resultObj.toString());
The above requests 1 Military and 2 Survivors, and 3 Gangers. To specify the gender of a request in a value object, append -M, -F, or -? to the end of the type. To specify a Star, use an asterisk for count: *xMilitary-F, *xGanger-M, *Police-?
The above requests a Female Star Military, a Male Star Ganger, and a Star Police of random gender where in half possibly are female. Not specifying a gender notation generates solely males.
