ATZ :: Roster Generation

This page generates a sample card sheet using an input field. Each card generated can be edited by double-clicking on it; a double-sized version will appear in a modal window. Fields that can be edited will highlight; click into a field to modify its values. To generate a cardsheet, enter one or more defintions in the field below. Here are some examples: 

6                          //Generate 6 random characters ... all Males
6x-?                       //Generate 6 random characters either Male or Female
1xMilitary                 //Generate 1 Military character
*xPolice                   //Generate a Star Police character
2xSurvivor-F               //Generate 2 Survivor characters - all Female
4xCivilian-?               //Generate 4 Civilian characters - up to half are Female
1xGanger, 2xMilitary       //Generate 1 Ganger and 2 Military characters

To properly view the generated cards, the "Lots of Dead Crack Babies" font will need to be installed into your system's "Fonts" folder. Download the font from here. Otherwise, the generated cards default to Google Fonts API for 'Metal Mania'.

Before printing, adjust the printer margins to 0.25 inch or 1 CM or greater. This will fix weird layout issue. Also, remove 'Shrink to Page'; cards are 2.5"x3.5". Do not print headers, footers, or other meta data on page. To save the image for later, select the printer as 'Adobe PDF' if Acrobat is installed.
